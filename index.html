About TWICE

TWICE is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment. Debuting in 2015, they have captured the hearts of ONCE with their catchy music and stunning performances.

Meet the Members

  • Nayeon

    • Im Na-yeon, known professionally as Nayeon, is a South Korean singer born on September 22, 1995, in Seoul. She is the eldest member and lead vocalist of the renowned K-pop girl group TWICE, formed by JYP Entertainment through the reality show "Sixteen" in 2015.Nayeon's journey with JYP Entertainment began in 2010 when she joined as a trainee after a successful audition. Initially slated to debut with the group 6mix, the project was eventually canceled. Her breakthrough came with TWICE's debut in 2015, where she quickly gained recognition for her vocal prowess and stage presence.In June 2022, Nayeon made her solo debut with the mini-album "IM NAYEON," which achieved significant success, charting at #7 on the Billboard 200. She followed this with her second EP, "NA," released in 2024.
